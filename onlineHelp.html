Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ page. Here, you find the answers for the most likely issues you may be facing. If not, please consult the user manual for a detailed explanation of how to use the Birthday Scheduler.

1. When I click the "Get Course ID" button, the "Course ID" sheet does not update. Why is this the case?

The "Get Course ID" function retrieves the list of active courses from Google Classroom for the logged-in teacher. Make sure that you are currently logged into the correct google account and are a teacher of the desired google classroom.
