Course ID Issues

1. When I click the "Get Course ID" button, the "Course ID" sheet does not update or it does not include the courseID of the classroom I want to post in. Why is this the case?

The "Get Course ID" button retrieves the list of active courses from Google Classroom that the Account is the owner or "teacher" of. Make sure that you are currently logged into the correct google account and are a "teacher" of the desired google classroom.

2. Will changing the "Message" affect the courseIDs in any way?

Changing the message will not impact the way the courseIDs are obtained. You are able to change the message to whatever is desired, however, it will appear before the names of the person. Make sure it is appropriate if wishing to say something other than "Happy Birthday".
